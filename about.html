About Myself

I was born in Shreveport, Louisianna, but lived overseas, specifically in China, for 10 years. I attended Boston University and majored in International Relations. For the past three years, I worked as a Customer Solutions Specialist in an insurance company called Plymouth Rock Assurance. My whole life I have had to constantly adapt to new situations. As a Korean growing up in many different countries overseas, I am what is considered a “third culture kid” and have had to learn to adapt and succeed in any environment. I was constantly immersing myself in different cultures, providing me with many perspectives and a global understanding from ayoung age. That experience has proven invaluable as I often apply this mindset to the companies I work for.

Outside of work, I am also on a dance team that gives me a space to explore my creativity through choreography, providing me the freedom to express myself through movement. Dance has taught me the significance of always being a student of the things I am passionate for. From watching videos and competitions on YouTube to spending countless hours training in fundamentals and applying those fundamentals in my movement, I find myself pursuing constant growth in this hobby, even seeking knowledge from many respected figures in the Hip Hop community to inspire and nurture my creative output.  Though I am coming from a non-coding background, I am confident that my qualities and experience will allow me to thrive at Coding Temple as well as the industry. Whenever there is a dream I want to attain, I will undoubtedly do whatever it takes to make it into a reality.
